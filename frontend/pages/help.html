FAQs

How do I reset my data?

Open the Profile card and click “Delete All Data”.

How are my plans stored?

All data lives inside the local /data folder as JSON.

How do notifications work?

When you save a reminder we send a browser notification via the service worker.

Where can I contact a trainer?

Use the Professionals section – each card includes call, email, and chat links.
